Hello there. You have stumbled upon Quinn's nascent github.io presence. Here's some stuff they've built.

  • Med Calc is a tool that projects how long your supply of injectable medication will last.
  • Better Late, Whatever. is a radio show they host on WXDU.
  • Last Week allows you to type in your last.fm username and then see at a glance who your friends have been listening to the most over the past seven days.
  • The Past Times of The Postmodern American Pastime will be a tool (possibly leveraging the cypher that is the ESPN fantasy sports API, or maybe just clunkily page-scraping) providing a detailed history of a truly iconic 10-team fantasy baseball league, soon beginning its 14th season.
